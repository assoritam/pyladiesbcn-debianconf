What is Pyladies?





An international mentorship group with a focus on helping more women become active participants and leaders in the Python open-source community





Pyladies Goals

  • Promote Python usage and community
    • Education
    • Conferences
    • Events
  • Provide a friendly support network for women
  • Promote PyLadies chapters creation and visibility
    • “PyLadies Handbook”
    • Web Page
    • Organizers mailing list

Pyladies Worldwide

25 chapters around the world and growing
